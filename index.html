Soft Skills Training Academy



About us


Established in 2015, WINGS – DESIRE TO FLY is one of the first Soft Skills Training Academy for kids in Surat. We have almost nutured 10000+ kids across the nation. Soft skills are skills related to human behavior and directed to emotional and social issues, improving the personality of each individual. By working on soft skills in the education of children from the early grades, they can learn to interact with others in a proactive, creative and empathetic way naturally. These skills even include social graces, communication abilities, language skills, personal habits, emotional empathy, time management, teamwork and leadership traits.



Our Values


We believe in honesty, transparency, gratitude and authenticity. Our courses are based on extended research and proven practices. We support kids to follow their own path of happiness by creating a safe space where all are welcome to learn, participate, explore and experience the power of SOFT SKILLS.



Vision


We aspire to be a leading Soft Skills Training Academy For Kids.


Mission


WINGS believes in giving a chance to your child for exploring the outer space of the world. We engage ourselves to fulfil the dream which you have seen for your child. It’s a platform to make your child believe ‘YES YOU CAN’.
